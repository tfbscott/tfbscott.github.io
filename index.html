About Me

Hello there! I am Scott Fado-Bristow, a current student at DigiPen Institute of Technology studying Computer Science and Game Design.

I have worked on multiple student game projects with teams ranging in size from 3 to 11 people. I have taken on numerous roles including Technical Director, Producer, Build Master, Technical Designer and Tools Programmer. Each project is a new challenge and opportunity to learn. Across these projects I have explored custom engine creation, procedural content generation, animation systems, custom editors, particle systems, scripting implementation, and so much more.

Video Games are my passion. They can provide experiences that range from masterful art pieces that will last for hours to small, 15-minute adventures that keep you coming back to play more. It is truly a genre like no other. I look forward to working in the industry and help expand it to new heights.
