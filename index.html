About Me

Hello there! I am Scott Fado-Bristow.

I am a huge fan of video games. My house is decorated with random statues and posters from game franchises, and my desk is always covered in old consoles. My free time is often spent playing board games or video games with my friends. I also enjoy painting miniatures, baking, and walking to nearby thrift stores.

Across these projects I explored custom engine creation, procedural content generation, animation systems, custom editors, particle systems, scripting implementation, and so much more. My projects helped land me my current position at Perfect Day, where each day I get to tackle programming challenges with a smile on my face.
