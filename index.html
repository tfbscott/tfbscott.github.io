About Me

Hello there! I am Scott Fado-Bristow, a current student a Digipen Institute of technology studying Computer Science and Game Design.

I have worked on multiple student game projects, with team sizes ranging from 3 to 11 people. Across these projects I have taken on multiple roles, including Technical Direction, Producer, Build Master and Tools Programmer. Each project is a new challenge and a new opportunity to learn something. Across these projects I have delved into custom engine creation, procedural content generation, animation systems, custom editors, particle systems, scripting implementation, and so much more!

Video Games are my passion. They can range from masterful art pieces that last hours to small 15-minute adventures you keep coming back to. It is truly a genre like no other and one I hope to help expand one day.
